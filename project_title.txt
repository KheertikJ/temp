Project title: DigiChords - facilitating guitar learning for beginners

The aim of this project is to develop a web application where users will be able to learn basics of playing guitar, which will encompass music theory as well. Users will be able to track their progress over time, . Furthermore, users will grow with an online community whereby they will be able to share their learning progress, and experiences achieved.
